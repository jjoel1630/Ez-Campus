What We Do

Ez-Campus is a free to use Chrome Extenstion that allows users to input their grades and calculate them ahead of time



How to Use Ez-Campus


Step 1: Choose the Assignment Category

From the dropdown, select the category for which you are entering the assignment.


Step 2: Enter the Assignment Name

Enter the name of the assignment in the form field.


Step 3: Enter Your Score

Enter the number of points you received on the assignment in the form field.


Step 4: Enter the Total Score

Enter the total number of points for the assignment in the form field.
