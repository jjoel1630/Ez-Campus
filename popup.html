What We Do

Infinite Campus is a website that allows students to view their grades, read emails, and track their service hours. However, many teachers are late with their grades and take a long time to upload them. In fact, some teachers publish grades only during the final week of school, inflicting immense amounts of stress on eager students who are nervous about their assignments. Ez-Campus is a free-to-use Google Chrome extension that allows Infinite Campus users to enter and calculate their new grades. Note: Grades posted on Ez-Campus are not affiliated with the actual grade posted by the teacher, it is in place to help the students get a better of idea what they must do in order to bring up their grade.

How to Use Ez-Campus

Step 1: Choose the Assignment Category

From the dropdown, select the category for which you are entering the assignment.


Step 2: Enter the Assignment Name

Enter the name of the assignment in the form field.


Step 3: Enter Your Score

Enter the number of points you received on the assignment in the form field.


Step 4: Enter the Total Score

Enter the total number of points for the assignment in the form field.
